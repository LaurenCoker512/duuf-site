Denton

Fellowship

We are a loving, diverse, open fellowship working together to inspire spiritual growth, encourage ethical actions, seek truth and improve the community and the world.

Welcome to DUUF!

Our church is about genuine inclusion, depth and joy, reason and spirit. We have been a voice of progressive religion in Denton since 1949, working toward a more just and compassionate world in all of what we do. We have been on the forefront of LGBTQ+ initiatives for decades.

Join us any Sunday at 9:30am or 11:30am for one of our services. And if you're ready to make the leap to membership, fill out a membership commitment form today!
